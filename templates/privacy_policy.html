Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Resume Data: When you upload or paste your resume, we process the text to generate job match scores.
  • Job Descriptions: We analyze job descriptions to match them against your resume.
  • Usage Data: We collect non-personal data such as IP address and browser type to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze resumes and job descriptions for skill matching.
  • To provide job recommendations based on resume content.
  • To improve and optimize the Resume Screening App.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with third parties. However, we may use external APIs for job recommendations.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at support@resumescreening.com.
